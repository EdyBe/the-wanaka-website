DIRECTOR OF FOOTBALL 
Wanaka FC · Full-time · Wanaka, New Zealand 
Coach the beautiful game in the most beautiful location.
Wanaka FC is seeking a Director of Football to lead our club at the highest level of regional football in New Zealand. This is a rare opportunity: a senior, paid coaching role in a thriving football club, set in Wanaka, one of the most spectacular places to live in the world.
About the Club
We are Wanaka FC, a club competing in the Southern League, with over 500 junior members, a year-round Academy programme, and men's teams. Since our founding in 2010, we have grown into one of the most ambitious football clubs in regional New Zealand, making history as the smallest town to field a team in the NZ National League.
At the heart of everything we do is player development. We believe that developing players the right way, technically, tactically, and as people, is the foundation of a great football club. We play a possession-based game, invest in educating our players at every level, and take pride in creating clear pathways from our junior programme through to the first team and beyond. Wanaka FC is a stepping stone to higher levels of football for local youth and visiting men's players, a responsibility we take seriously. 
The Role
As Director of Football, you will have oversight of all on-field activity at the club, the driving force behind our football identity, from the men's first team to our youngest players.
Your responsibilities will include:
Head Coach of the Wanaka FC men's first team in the Southern League
Running and coaching within the year-round Academy programme, developing the next generation of players
Creating and implementing clear player development pathways from junior football through to the senior men's team 
Directing, mentoring and educating coaches within the club
Overseeing and leading the club's year-round football programmes across junior, youth and senior football
Contributing to the strategic development of football at Wanaka FC
Ideally, you will start the role in October, 2026
What We Offer
