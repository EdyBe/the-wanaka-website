April Holiday Programme

Two days of action-packed football fun for players aged U8–U12

Keep the Momentum Going!

Join our April School Holiday Football Programme! Keep the momentum going these school holidays with two days of action-packed football fun.

Designed for players (boys and girls) aged U8–U12, our holiday programme is the perfect way to stay sharp, build new skills, and enjoy the game in a fun, grassroots-focused environment.

With the season kicking off in Term 2, this is a great chance to keep up the progress made in pre-season — so you're ready to hit the ground running when games start!

Expect engaging games, exciting challenges, and plenty of FUN!

  • Boys & girls welcome
  • Ages U8 to U12
  • Fun, skills-focused sessions

Programme Details

  • Dates: 8 and 9 April
  • Location: Kelly's Flat
  • Time: 9:00am – 12:00noon
  • Ages: U8 – U12 (boys & girls)
  • Cost: $50 per day  |  $90 for both days
