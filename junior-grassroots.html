About

Recreational football - Mixed and girls-only

Wānaka FC runs grassroots football for U5 till U15 during the term 2, 3, and 4 school term. The emphasis is on fun and ensuring players have an enjoyable experience while also learning new skills.

  • U5 to U15
  • Mixed & girls-only
  • Term 2, 3 and 4 programs

Term 4 Summer Football

Register Now!

We're all about having a blast on the field with this fun, social, game-based program. This is your chance to play, get creative, and hone your skills in a super supportive environment. Whether you want to join our Mixed group or the Girls-Only group, there's a spot for everyone!

  • When: Each Wednesday after school
  • Dates: October 8 - November 26
  • Time: 3:45 till 4:45pm
  • Location: Kelly’s Flat
  • Age groups: U6-U12 Mixed & U6-U12 Girls-Only

Term 2 Season

  • The club’s grassroots season occurs every Sunday during term 2, offering mixed grades from U5 till U15, and girls-only football for U6 till U12.
  • Training starts in March, with the ten-game season running from April till June.
  • U5-U8 - the younger ages play inhouse football every Sunday morning at Kelly’s Flat. This means young players don’t need to leave Wānaka to enjoy their football.
  • U9-U15 - Wānaka teams play against Queenstown, Cromwell and Alexandra, with all teams travelling to one central location weekly. Approximately five of the ten games are played in Wānaka.

Term 3 Futsal and Street Football

The term 3 grassroots program sees U6-U9 players enjoying futsal, while U10-13 players play street football on the hockey turf. Both programs have a ‘let them play’ philosophy that is designed to be fun, engaging and game-based. Players are organised into teams on the day, and have a series of small-sided games
